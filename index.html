A New Paradigm in Newtonian Mechanics

The Carrier Feedback Acceleration Drive (CFAD) demonstrates how a specific kinematic coupling can lock a mechanical system into a state of inherent dynamic imbalance. The only consistent physical response is continuous acceleration, governed by the geometry and magnitude of built-in tension‚Äîrather than any external energy input. This does not violate physics; instead, it deepens our understanding of it, introducing a new paradigm for motion and energy in closed mechanical systems.
