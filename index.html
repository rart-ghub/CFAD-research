THE CORE DISCOVERY

  • Kinematic Lock: The specific arrangement of gears and chains creates a closed loop in which the tension force becomes permanently trapped ‚Äì there is no path for relaxation.
  • Built-in Tension as the Driver: A constant force F0 cannot dissipate, so the only possible response of the system is ‚Äì continuous acceleration. Its magnitude is determined by geometry, not by energy input.
  • Negative Dynamic Contribution of the Carrier: The carrier behaves as an energy source: instead of decelerating the system, it accelerates in the opposite direction, delivering energy to the rest of the mechanism.
  • Dynamic Inertial Equilibrium: The static reaction of the fixed sun gear is balanced solely by the inertial forces of the accelerating masses ‚Äì an equilibrium that sustains itself through acceleration.
  • Experimental Proof: In the mixed mode (simultaneous action of built-in tension and external torques), a measurable interaction term appears in the power balance ‚Äì a direct confirmation of the phenomenon.
